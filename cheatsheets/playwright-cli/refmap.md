## CLI 命令面

- `--help` 总览与子命令帮助：`npx @playwright/cli@latest --help`
- `open` 帮助：`npx @playwright/cli@latest --help open`
- `snapshot` 帮助：`npx @playwright/cli@latest --help snapshot`
- `install` 帮助：`npx @playwright/cli@latest --help install`
